Our Legacy

Founded in 1961 in the heart of Aminabad, Badri Narain Laxmi Narain Jewellers is more than a store; it's a tradition. For over six decades, we have served the people of Lucknow with integrity, ensuring that every gram of gold sold is pure and authentic.

Under the able management of Prateek Gupta and Pranay Gupta, the legacy has been modernized while keeping its soul intact. We bring you the latest designs with the same old-world trust.
